HIYH Website

Health in Your Hands is a software-based communication system that revolutionizes the way people care for their health. They needed a more clean, simple approach that conveys that they are a tech solution. I updated their website with a more modern look and also designed a new logo for them. The swish in the logo gives the impression of a hand and symbolizes holding health in your hands.
