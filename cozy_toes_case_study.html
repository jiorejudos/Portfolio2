About the Project

The goal of this project was to design a responsive shopping experience. This shop sells fun, fashionable socks.

Competitive Analysis

I analyzed three different online stores – The Sock Drawer, Sock It To Me, Happy Socks – to see what kind of features they offered.


Strengths
- The Sock Drawer has good filter options to help you find what you want. Happy Socks and Sock It to Me both had an easily checkout process.



Weaknesses
- The Sock Drawer has lots of steps to the checkout process. Sock It To Me and Happy Socks could use better search filter options.



Opportunities
- All the sites had lots of fun sock, but not a lot of atheletic or dress socks.



Threats
- A shop that sells fun socks as well as athletic and dressy socks.


From this research, I learned that there is a market for a site that has a variety of socks and good search filters to help you find what you want.

Style Choices

When building The Cozy Toes look, I wanted it to fell warm and comfortable. I choose an analogous color scheme with warm colors to show that harmony. The logo uses the Poplar font because it is a good display face with lots of simplicity.

User Testing

As I created the prototypes and went through usability testing, I found that users liked the font, the name and logo. Generally, people seemed to say that it was fairly simple and easy to use. Some test participants were confused about all the website could do, which points to a need to have a detailed FAQ section for new and potential users.

Design

The goal for the NoteNerd design was to show professionalism and efficiency. I wanted the the web app to be clean and easy to use.

What I learned doing this project?

It was surprising that people didn’t really think about the issue of organizing their bookmarks and notes more. I feel like showing the features really made people see the benefits of the app. In surveying potential users, I think a search function is really a critical feature to add to the app in the future. One of the potential issues in creating another note-taking app was standing out from the competition. However, this product differentiates itself with its ability to better organize different types of information.

In the second version, I would add the ability to mark content as a favorite, share on social media and add collaborators. For future versions, I would like to add the ability to set reminders, clip screenshots, a search function and speech-to-text for note-taking.
