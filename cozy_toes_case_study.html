About the Project

The goal of this project was to design a responsive shopping experience. This shop sells fun, fashionable socks.

Competitive Analysis

I analyzed three different online stores – The Sock Drawer, Sock It To Me, Happy Socks – to see what kind of features they offered.


Strengths
- The Sock Drawer has good filter options to help you find what you want. Happy Socks and Sock It to Me both had an easily checkout process.



Weaknesses
- The Sock Drawer has lots of steps to the checkout process. Sock It To Me and Happy Socks could use better search filter options.



Opportunities
- All the sites had lots of fun sock, but not a lot of atheletic or dress socks.



Threats
- A shop that sells fun socks as well as athletic and dressy socks.


From this research, I learned that there is a market for a site that has a variety of socks and good search filters to help you find what you want.

Style Choices

When building The Cozy Toes look, I wanted it to fell warm and comfortable. I choose an analogous color scheme with warm colors to show that harmony. The logo uses the Poplar font because it is a good display face with lots of simplicity.

Wireframes

Then, I started arranging the the elements in the wireframes.

User Testing

As I created the prototypes and went through usability testing, I found that users liked the tagline and that there were a lot of images of different socks on the homepage. It was very clear from the beginning what the shop sold. I also received feedback that many shoppers liked to have a quick add feature. This was they could easily add something to the cart without anymore steps. So I added that to the final product.

Design

The goal for the Cozy Toes design was to show a simple comfortable look with lots of white space.

Project Lessons

I learned the importance of surveying users before the process. I wish I would have done a survey of users preferences for the checkout process. I think the checkout process turned out well, but it would have been better to get that feedback at the beginning to make sure that it was clear.

In the second version, I would like to add a link to the sock brand on the product detail page. So, if a user has a brand they prefer, they can find other socks of the same brand.
