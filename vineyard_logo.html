Cville Vineyard Redesign

The Charlottesville Vineyard wanted to update their logo and website. They were looking for a logo with a nice icon that they could use for branding. I came up with a simple icon that would be reconizable to their audience. They were also looking to modernize their site. The new template includes a parallax scrolling effect to engage the viewer. I also included more local photography to really show who they are as a church.
